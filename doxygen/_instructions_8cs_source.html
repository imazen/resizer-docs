00026          * fi.scale - Specific to two very infrequently used plugins: FreeImageBuilder and FreeImageResizer
00027          * preservePalette - Specific to the PrettyGifs plugin, and not well tested. *TODO*
00028          * progressive - Specific to the FreeImage pipeline - not supported by GDI, WIC, or WPF
00029          * shadow* - I'm hoping to retire/replace the DropShadow plugin sometime. I think it is very infrequently used.
00030          * memcache - This feature is pre-alpha
